### Category Performance

| Category | Sales |
|----------|-------|
| Technology | $836,154 |
| Furniture | $742,000 |
| Office Supplies | $719,047 |

> **Insight:** Technology generates the highest sales, making it the strongest performing category. It should be prioritized for growth and investment.

---

### Regional Performance

| Region | Sales |
|--------|-------|
| West | $725,458 |
| East | $678,781 |
| Central | $501,240 |
| South | $391,722 |

> **Insight:** The business performs strongest in the West, suggesting higher demand or better market penetration there. The South region has the lowest sales performance.

---

### Top Products

- The **Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier** is the top-performing product with $61,600 in sales.
- Several high-performing products belong to the Technology category.

> **Insight:** A small group of high-value products contributes significantly to overall revenue.

---

### Sales Trend Over Time

| Year | Sales |
|------|-------|
| 2014 | $484,247 |
| 2015 | $470,533 |
| 2016 | $609,206 |
| 2017 | $733,215 |

> **Insight:** Sales declined slightly from 2014 to 2015, then showed strong and consistent growth from 2015 to 2017. The business demonstrates a clear upward trend after 2015.

---

### Seasonality & Demand Patterns

- Sales consistently **peak in September, November, and December**.
- The **last quarter of the year (Q4)** shows the highest sales activity.
- Sales are consistently **lower in Q1** at the beginning of each year.

> **Insight:** There is strong seasonal demand toward the end of the year, likely driven by holiday purchasing activity and year-end procurement cycles.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. **Focus on Technology Products**
   The Technology category generates the highest revenue. Increasing inventory, expanding marketing efforts, and diversifying product variety could drive further growth.

2. **Leverage High-Performance Regions**
   The West and East regions show the strongest sales performance. Expanding operations, promotions, and potential partnerships in these regions can maximize returns.

3. **Improve Underperforming Regions**
   The South region has the lowest sales. The business should investigate causes such as low demand, pricing issues, or limited distribution channels.

4. **Capitalize on Seasonal Demand**
   Sales consistently peak in Q4. The business should increase stock levels, launch targeted marketing campaigns, and run promotions during this period.

5. **Optimize Top Products Strategy**
   A small number of products contribute significantly to total revenue. These products should be prioritized in marketing and supply chain decisions to protect and grow that revenue stream.
